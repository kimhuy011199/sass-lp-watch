It’s always looking out for you.

01. Blood oxygen. Key to your wellness.

Apple Watch Series 6 features a revolutionary sensor and app to measure your blood oxygen levels, an important indicator of your overall wellness. The Blood Oxygen app can allow you to measure the oxygen level of your blood on-demand directly from your wrist.

Learn how to use Blood Oxygen App →

02. ECG on your wrist. Anytime, anywhere.

With the ECG app, Apple Watch is capable of generating an ECG similar to a single-lead electrocardiogram. It’s a momentous achievement for a wearable device that can provide critical data for doctors and peace of mind. By looking at an ECG, a doctor can gain insights about your heart rhythm.

Learn how to use ECG App →

03. It has your heart in mind.

Unusually high or low heart rates and irregular heart rhythms (known as arrhythmias) could be signs of a serious condition.3 But many people don't recognize the symptoms, so the underlying causes often go undiagnosed. Notifications in the Heart Rate app will alert you to these irregularities.

Learn more about Heart Notifications →

04. Stay well-versed in your wellness.

Apple Watch has apps that help you focus on your overall health and wellness by letting you easily track your menstrual cycle, protect your hearing from loud sounds, and practice mindfulness.It can even remind you to wash them right when you get home.

Learn how to use Cycle Tracking →
